Our Story

A Love Letter to Balinese Coffee Culture

Born from a deep passion for Indonesian coffee heritage, Kopi Dewata was founded in the heart of Ubud by a Balinese family dedicated to sharing the island's finest flavors with the world.

Every cup tells a story — from the volcanic highlands of Kintamani where our beans are harvested, to the careful roasting process guided by generations of knowledge. We believe coffee is not just a drink, it's a sacred ritual.
